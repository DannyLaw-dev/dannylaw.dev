About

I build software in my spare time — games, tools, and creative web apps. By day I work in retail at Wex, a UK photo and video shop, where I also build internal tools to make our workflows less painful.

Outside work I run D&D campaigns and build the things they need. Most of what's in the workshop right now is D&D-adjacent — campaign companions, character sheets, world-building tools.

The workshop itself is a personal cloud dev environment — a Hetzner VM running code-server in the browser, Caddy reverse-proxying everything, and every project deploying itself on push to main. The point isn't the infra; the point is shipping small things often.
